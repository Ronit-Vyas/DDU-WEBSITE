DDU Admissions 2025

Empowering Minds, Building Futures

Programs Offered

  • B.Tech: Computer, Mechanical, IT, Civil, Chemical and EC
  • M.Tech: Various Engineering Specializations
  • MBA / MCA
  • Ph.D.: Engineering, Sciences, and Management

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.Tech: 10+2 (PCM) + GUJCET (ACPC Rank / JEE Rank)
  • M.Tech: B.E/B.Tech + GATE (optional)
  • MBA/MCA: Any Graduate + CMAT
  • Ph.D.: PG Degree + Entrance Test

Admission Process

  1. Register via ACPC or DDU Admission Portal
  2. Fill Application Form and Upload Documents
  3. Participate in Counseling / Interviews
  4. Fee Payment & Seat Confirmation

Important Dates

  • Application Opens: May 15, 2025
  • First Round Counseling: June 10, 2025
  • Session Starts: July 15, 2025
