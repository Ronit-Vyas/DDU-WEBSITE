Admission Information

Programs Offered: B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, MCA, Ph.D. ,B.Pharm ,M.Pharm ,MBBS ,BHMS

Eligibility:

  • For B.Tech: 10+2 with PCM + GUJCET
  • For M.Tech: Relevant UG + GATE (preferred)
  • MBA/MCA: Graduation + CMAT

Admission Process:

  1. Apply via ACPC portal or DDU admission site : ACPC Portal
  2. Appear for counseling at DDU
  3. Seat allocation & confirmation

Important Dates:

  • Application Opens: May 15
  • First Round Counseling: June 10
  • Session Starts: July 15
